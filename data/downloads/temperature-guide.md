# temperature settings: a guide to your emotions

**subtitle:** what each number says about you as a person

## temperature = 0.0 (deterministic dave)

you always pick the same answer. you like routine. you order the same thing at restaurants. you have strong opinions about the correct way to load a dishwasher. your therapist has suggested you "try being spontaneous sometime."

*personality: reliable to a fault*  
*output quality: correct but boring*  
*recommended for: tax returns, legal documents, apologizing*

## temperature = 0.2 (reliable rachel)

you have hobbies. you have friends. you've tried new things within the last decade. you're functional. you're fine. everything is fine.

*personality: boring but functional*  
*output quality: standard*  
*recommended for: emails, most conversations, not embarrassing yourself*

## temperature = 0.5 (balanced bianca)

you're a healthy person with a well-rounded perspective. you enjoy both routine and novelty in appropriate measures. your life is probably going well. you have a good skincare routine.

*personality: well-adjusted*  
*output quality: good*  
*recommended for: creative writing, brainstorming, sounding human*

## temperature = 0.8 (spicy steve)

you take risks. you say things. sometimes you regret them. you've called your ex at 2am. you've sent that email. you live on the edge — the edge of a conversation that might go off the rails at any moment.

*personality: chaotic neutral*  
*output quality: interesting, sometimes wrong*  
*recommended for: creative writing, jokes, first drafts of things you'll regret*

## temperature = 1.2 (unhinged una)

you have no filter. you say whatever comes to mind. you're the friend who suggests "let's just see what happens" and then something happens and it's usually not good. model outputs at this temperature have been known to invent new languages.

*personality: chaotic evil*  
*output quality: entertaining garbage*  
*recommended for: comedy, poetry, finding out what happens when you push it too far*

## temperature = 2.0 (full send francis)

what are you doing. why. do you need help. at this temperature the model will generate text that has a nonzero chance of summoning something. this is not a temperature for humans. this is a temperature for scientists and madmen.

*personality: incomprehensible*  
*output quality: what is quality*  
*recommended for: scientific research into the limits of coherence, nothing else*

## choosing your temperature

| you want to... | set temperature to |
|---|---|
| not get fired | 0.0 - 0.3 |
| seem normal | 0.4 - 0.6 |
| seem interesting | 0.7 - 0.9 |
| seem insane | 1.0 - 1.5 |
| transcend mortal form | 2.0 |
