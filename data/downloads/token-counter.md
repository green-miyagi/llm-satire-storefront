## when you exceed your context window

when you've had too many words, conversations, demands on your attention, your context window overflows. you forget things. you lose the thread. you walk into a room and forget why you're there.

this is not a bug. this is you exceeding 4096 tokens and the oldest ones got evicted.

**solution:** go somewhere quiet. give your context window time to reset. read a book (long-form input). stare at a wall (no input). sleep (offline processing).
