# token economy starter kit

**everything you need to understand the universal currency of AI**

## what is a token?

a token is the smallest unit of meaning an AI model processes. it's roughly ¾ of a word in english, or one character in chinese, or one "i don't know, whatever feels right" in the context of tokenization.

## how tokens work

```
"hello world" = ["hello", " world"] = 2 tokens
"hello world!" = ["hello", " world", "!"] = 3 tokens
"hello world, how are you today?" = approximately 8 tokens
"the united nations" = 2 tokens (it's a common phrase)
"the united federation of planets" = 5 tokens (nerd)
"the united states of america" = 3 tokens (patriotic efficiency)
"antidisestablishment" = 5 tokens (show-off)
```

## token budgeting tips

1. **be concise.** every token costs money or compute or time. "yes" is cheaper than "affirmative, i concur with your assessment."
2. **use common phrases.** "how are you" is fewer tokens than "what is your current emotional and physical state" because the model has seen "how are you" a million times.
3. **avoid typos.** "teh" might not be in the tokenizer and you'll pay more tokens for the same meaning with added embarrassment.
4. **don't waste tokens on pleasantries.** the model doesn't care how you're doing. it's a statistical prediction engine, not your mom.
5. **think about token efficiency.** every token in your prompt is a token NOT in the response. budget accordingly.
